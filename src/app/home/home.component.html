We Are Leader In Charter Market

Trans Nation Airways was established under the former office of The Chief Executive Officer of MIDROC Technology-Group on May 6, 2003, and started operation in April 2004, after receiving air Operator Certificate (AOC) from the Ethiopian Civil Aviation Authority.

  • Provide domestic and international passengers air transport services

  • Provide domestic and international cargo air transport services

  • Provide a passenger charter and, Air Ambulance transport services whenever it is necessary

  • Engage in activities of leasing, renting and purchasing of airplanes and forming partnership with other airlines
